Viewing FAQs

How do I view the wedding?

No app download or login needed! Just come back here on our wedding day and once the ceremony starts, the video will automatically play.

What should I wear?

The video steam will be one-way, which means while you will be able to see us, no one will be able to see you, so you can even watch us get married from your bed!

How do I pass along congratulations?

There will be a digital guestbook and live chat option available during the ceremony, so feel free to drop a note for us to read after the ceremony!

How long do you expect the ceremony to take?

It should be about 30-45 minutes.

If I can't watch the wedding live, can I view it after the fact?

Yep! We'll have the video here for you to watch afterwards.
